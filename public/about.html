Subject: Claws Kitten

D.O.B: 30 September 2011

Claws "Boots" Kitten was born in a horse barn in rural Minnesota. She was adopted by her "hooman," Alex Ogden, in November 2011, and has since relocated to Israel.

Claws began her career as a private investigator in 2012 following the disappearance of her beloved toy mouse, Cheddar. (See Case #0001)
