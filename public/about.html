Subject: Claws Kitten

D.O.B: 30 September 2011

Claws "Boots" Kitten was born in a horse barn in rural Minnesota. She adopted her "hooman," Alex Ogden, in November 2011, and together they relocated to Israel in 2015. Since moving across the Atlantic, Claws has kindly taken in another "hooman," called Gili, along with a roguish runaway street kitten named Caraway.

Claws began her career as a private investigator in 2012 following the disappearance of her beloved toy mouse, Cheddar. (See Case #0001) Claws is fully licensed as a freelance Private Investigator by the State of Catatonia.
